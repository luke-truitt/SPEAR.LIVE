Ethan Astrachan
Duke University Class of 2021 || Computer Science and Political Science
Ethan is a sophomore at Duke University passionate about applying technology to create more efficient policy solutions. He works as a Research Assistant, using natural word processing to help predict civil crackdowns in partnership with USAID, and as Treasurer for Camp Kesem at Duke University, a non-profit serving children affected by a parents cancer. He has previously worked on a congressional campaign assisting with fundraising, and will spend this summer at Kessel Run, helping deploy software for the Air Force.
